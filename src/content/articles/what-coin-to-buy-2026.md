## 1. Liquidity: The Foundation of Any Serious Project

Liquidity determines stability.

On networks like Solana, tokens trade in liquidity pools (for example, SOL paired with the token). If liquidity is shallow, small trades cause large price swings. That creates fragility.

A stronger project shows:

- Visible liquidity on-chain  
- Consistent trading environment  
- No hidden pool manipulation  

**RROTA ($RTA)** operates with live on-chain liquidity on Solana, allowing transparent verification through public explorers and DEX analytics platforms.

Liquidity size alone doesn’t guarantee success — but lack of liquidity almost guarantees instability.

## 2. Token Control: Mint and Freeze Authority

One of the most important checks when evaluating what coin to buy in 2026 is token control.

On Solana, every token has:

- Mint authority (ability to create new tokens)
- Freeze authority (ability to freeze wallets)

If these are not revoked, supply can theoretically be altered.

**RROTA has both mint and freeze authority revoked.**

This means:
- No additional tokens can be minted.
- No wallet can be arbitrarily frozen.
- Supply mechanics are fixed.

In a market where trust is everything, this matters.
