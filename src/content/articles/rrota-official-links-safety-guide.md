## Common Crypto Scams to Avoid

Crypto projects often attract scammers. Users should stay careful.

Common scam methods include:

- Fake Telegram admins
- Fake support accounts
- Fake airdrop links
- Fake token contracts
- Fake wallet connection pages
- Fake reward claims
- Fake “urgent verification” messages
- Fake investment managers
- Fake presale links
- Fake screenshots
- Fake websites with similar domains

If something feels rushed, secret, or too good to be true, stop and verify.
